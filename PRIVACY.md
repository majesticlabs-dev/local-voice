# Privacy Policy

**Local Voice** — by Majestic Labs LLC, Austin, TX
**Effective date:** April 10, 2026
**Website:** [https://majesticlabs.dev](https://majesticlabs.dev)

## Summary

Local Voice runs entirely on your machine. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data.

## What We Collect

Nothing. Local Voice performs all text-to-speech processing locally on your device. No text, audio, usage data, analytics, telemetry, or personal information is sent to any external server.

## Network Activity

- The TTS service runs on `localhost` (127.0.0.1) and is not accessible from the internet.
- The optional "Server mode" in the desktop app exposes the API to your local network only — this is user-initiated and clearly disclosed in the app.
- The Chrome extension communicates exclusively with the local TTS service on your machine.
- No data is sent to Majestic Labs or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

Local Voice does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

All cached audio files are stored locally in the application directory on your machine. No data is stored remotely.

## Chrome Extension

The Local Voice Reader extension requires the following permissions:
- **activeTab / scripting**: To read the text content of the current page for speech synthesis.
- **storage**: To persist your voice and speed preferences locally in Chrome.
- **contextMenus**: To add a "Read aloud" option to the right-click menu.
- **offscreen**: To play audio in the background.

These permissions are used solely for local text-to-speech functionality. No browsing data is collected or transmitted.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this document and the effective date above. Given the local-only nature of this software, we do not anticipate collecting data in the future.
